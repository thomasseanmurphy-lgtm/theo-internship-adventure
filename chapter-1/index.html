📖 The story

Read me first!

Théo is a first-year logistics student. He needs an internship for the summer, so he wants to find the best company possible. This morning, he walks around the industrial park and looks at every warehouse.

Some companies look run-down: old trucks, broken windows and a messy yard. Then Théo sees a brand-new warehouse. It is bigger and more modern than the others. Fast robots move the pallets, the loading dock is tidy, and the staff look organized and friendly.

Théo takes a deep breath. He walks up to a supervisor and speaks clearly and politely. The company has looked for trainees since March, so the supervisor is happy. Théo has made a good impression, and yesterday he printed a fresh CV — so he leaves it and goes home to prepare. 📄
